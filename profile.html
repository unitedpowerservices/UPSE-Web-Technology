PROFILE OF UPS&E

United power service/Engineering, Construction, and Electricals is a business established from three business enterprises: (FutureCom, UC International and Index Enterprises) where Mohamed Mansaray was the technical engineer working on contracts since October 2013 and then later register the business in accordance with the business registration act of the republic of Sierra Leone.It was formed as a result of unsatisfactory work done by some technical engineers recommended by the above mentioned enterprises to undertake construction and installation process for their customers. The objectives of the formation of UPS&E is to carryout standardize engineering contracts such as: Construction, Solar Installation & Power Backup systems, residential and business premises wiring, fiber networking, wireless and local area networking, M & E Services, Generator and Air conditioning maintenance to meet customers’ specifications.
